  • WasteWatcher is designed to prevent food waste.
  • We need your help to reduce the food waste in our university. With a few simple clicks, you will be able save our environment and get a free meal at the end of it!
  • All you have to do is answer whether you will be dining at the Curve daily (mon-fri), if so, choose what meal you want.
  • By completing this by 8am, you wil earn 25 points, by 10 am you will earn 15 points. Even if you say no you will earn 10 points
  • Once you have reached 500 points you will be able to have a free meal at the Curve!
